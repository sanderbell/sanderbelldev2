## How It Works — The Launchpad

### $990 · One week · Idea to working product

No jargon. No agency black box. No surprises.

**1. We talk through your idea**
A real conversation — not a form to fill out. We figure out what the product actually is, who it's for, and what matters for version one. You'll leave with more clarity than you've had in months.

**2. I write your product blueprint**
A plain-English document mapping out features, flows, and priorities. No technical language. Something you can read, keep, and hand to anyone who works on it after me.

**3. I build your first version**
A real, working app — not a sketch, not a concept. Something you can open, use, and show to people you trust to get real feedback.

**4. You get a clear path forward**
How to improve it, how to find your first users, how to keep building — with or without me. You won't be handed something you don't understand.

**5. I'm with you the whole week**
Questions answered. Decisions guided. The thing most people fear — paying and then being left alone with something confusing — doesn't happen here.

### You walk away with:

A working product · A blueprint you own · Confidence that your idea is real · A concrete next step
